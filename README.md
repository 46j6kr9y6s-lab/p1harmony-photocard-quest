# P1ece Collector - P1Harmony Game 🎮📦

A fun collectible card game inspired by the P1Harmony fandom! Progress through 1000 levels, open boxes to collect photocards with different rarities, and level up your P1ece status!

## 🎮 Game Features

- **1000 Levels** divided into 5 tiers:
  - **Baby P1ece** (Levels 1-200) - Start your journey
  - **New P1ece** (Levels 201-400) - Growing collector
  - **P1ece** (Levels 401-600) - Experienced collector
  - **Legendary P1ece** (Levels 601-800) - Expert collector
  - **OG P1ece** (Levels 801-1000) - Ultimate collector

- **Collectible Photocards** with 6 rarity levels:
  - ⚪ **Common** (50% chance)
  - 🔵 **Rare** (30% chance)
  - 🟣 **Epic** (15% chance)
  - 🔴 **Mythic** (3% chance)
  - 🟡 **Legendary** (1.5% chance)
  - 💎 **Ultra Legendary** (0.5% chance)

- **Box Opening Mechanic**: Open up to 5 boxes per level to collect photocards
- **Level Progression**: Collect 3 photocards to complete each level
- **Progress Tracking**: Visual progress bar and tier badge updates
- **Local Save**: Your progress is saved in browser storage

## 🚀 How to Play

1. Open `index.html` in your browser
2. Click on the 📦 boxes to open them and reveal photocards
3. Collect 3 photocards to complete the current level
4. Click "Next Level →" to advance
5. Progress through all 1000 levels to become an OG P1ece!

## 📁 Files

- `index.html` - Game structure and HTML
- `style.css` - Beautiful styling and animations
- `script.js` - Game logic and mechanics
- `README.md` - This file

## 💾 Save System

Your progress is automatically saved to your browser's local storage. You can close the game and your level progress will be preserved!

## 🎨 Customization

You can easily customize:
- Level ranges for each tier (edit `TIERS` in `script.js`)
- Rarity drop rates (edit `RARITIES` in `script.js`)
- Number of cards to collect per level (change `CARDS_TO_COLLECT`)
- Colors and styling (edit `style.css`)

## 📱 Responsive Design

The game works on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices!
